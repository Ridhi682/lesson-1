Facts about Elephants

Elephants are truly remarkable creatures, known for their impressive size and intelligence. As the largest land mammals, African elephants can weigh up to 14,000 pounds, with males being the most massive. Despite their size, elephants are incredibly social and live in tight-knit family groups, led by a matriarch, the oldest female
